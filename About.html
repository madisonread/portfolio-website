My Story

Nice to meet you, I'm Madison!

I am a UI/UX designer with a background in visual design. As a designer, I am skilled at empathizing with people and creating user-centric design solutions. Being heavily involved with several entrepreneurial organizations and start-ups, I have an innovative spirit and enjoy a challenge! My passion to help people and my love for design are what drives me to create user interfaces that are simple, aesthetically pleasing, and data-driven. Motivated by innovation and adaptability, I can implement new ideas and processes quickly and effectively. I am energized by a collaborative and creative environment.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy other creative outlets such as painting, drawing and baking! I also love spending time with friends, being outdoors, hiking and going to the beach.
